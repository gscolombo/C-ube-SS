Tesseract

A tesseract is a four-dimensional geometric shape, also known as a hypercube. It is the four-dimensional analogue of a cube, extending the concept of three-dimensional space into the fourth dimension. Just as a cube consists of six square faces, a tesseract is composed of eight cubical cells. While it's challenging to visualize directly, analogies and mathematical representations help grasp its abstract nature.
