Dice

A dice is a small, cubical object typically used in games of chance and strategy. Each face of the die is marked with a different number of dots or pips, usually ranging from one to six. When rolled, the outcome is determined by the face that lands facing upwards, providing an element of randomness essential for various games and activities.
